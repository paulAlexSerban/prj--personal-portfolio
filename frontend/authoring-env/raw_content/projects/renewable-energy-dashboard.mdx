## Description:

A renewable energy dashboard allows users to track and analyze data related to the production and consumption of renewable energy sources, such as solar, wind, and hydro power. This project demonstrates your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application, integrating various components to enhance user experience and promote ecological awareness.

## Features:

-   Data Grid: Display a list of renewable energy projects, with relevant details such as project type, location, capacity, and completion date. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
-   Charts: Visualize renewable energy data using various charts, including bar charts for production trends, line charts for capacity growth, and pie charts for renewable energy source distribution.
-   Scheduler: Allow users to schedule maintenance activities, project milestones, and reminders related to renewable energy investments.
-   Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering renewable energy projects and scheduling events.
-   Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing renewable energy project descriptions, research findings, and investment opportunities, with formatting options, images, and tables.
