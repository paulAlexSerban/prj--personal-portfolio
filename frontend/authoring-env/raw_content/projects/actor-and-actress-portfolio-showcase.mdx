## Description:
An actor and actress portfolio showcase platform allows artists to create and maintain their online portfolios, including their filmography, photos, and demo reels. This project showcases your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application, integrating various components to enhance user experience and promote the world of art, actors, and theatres.

## Features:

- Data Grid: Display a list of actors and actresses, with relevant details such as name, filmography, and awards. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
- Charts: Visualize actor and actress data using various charts, including bar charts for awards and nominations, line charts for career progression, and pie charts for genre distribution.
- Scheduler: Allow users to schedule auditions, rehearsals, and reminders related to acting opportunities and career management.
- Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering actor and actress data, audition dates, and scheduled events.
- Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing actor and actress biographies, personal statements, and project descriptions, with formatting options, images, and tables.
