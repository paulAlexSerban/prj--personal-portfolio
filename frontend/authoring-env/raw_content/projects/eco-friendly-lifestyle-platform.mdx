## Description:
An eco-friendly lifestyle platform provides users with resources, tips, and tools to adopt sustainable practices in their daily lives. This project showcases your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application, integrating various components to enhance user experience and promote ecological awareness.

## Features:

- Data Grid: Display a list of eco-friendly products, practices, and resources, with relevant details such as category, impact, and cost. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
- Charts: Visualize eco-friendly data using various charts, including bar charts for carbon footprint reduction, line charts for resource consumption, and pie charts for eco-friendly category distribution.
- Scheduler: Allow users to schedule reminders for sustainable practices, such as recycling pick-up, composting, and energy-saving activities.
- Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering eco-friendly resources and scheduling events.
- Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing eco-friendly tips, product reviews, and personal sustainability plans, with formatting options, images, and tables.
