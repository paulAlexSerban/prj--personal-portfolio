## Description:
A job recruitment platform connects job seekers with potential employers and streamlines the hiring process. This project showcases your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application, integrating various components to enhance user experience.

## Features:

- Data Grid: Display a list of job postings or candidates, with relevant details such as job title, company, location, and required skills. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
- Charts: Visualize job-related data using various charts, including bar charts for open positions, line charts for application trends, and pie charts for industry distribution.
- Scheduler: Allow users to schedule interviews, deadlines, and reminders related to the recruitment process.
- Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering job postings or candidate applications.
- Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing job descriptions, resumes, and cover letters, with formatting options, images, and tables."
