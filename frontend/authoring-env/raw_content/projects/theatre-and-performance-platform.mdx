title: "Theatre and Performance Platform"
---

## Description:
A theatre and performance platform allows users to discover, rate, and share information about plays, performances, and local theatres. This project demonstrates your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application, integrating various components to enhance user experience and promote the world of art, actors, and theatres.

## Features:

- Data Grid: Display a list of plays, performances, and theatres, with relevant details such as title, director, cast, location, and user ratings. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
- Charts: Visualize theatre and performance data using various charts, including bar charts for attendance trends, line charts for ticket sales, and pie charts for genre distribution.
- Scheduler: Allow users to schedule events, shows, and reminders related to theatre performances and experiences.
- Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering plays, performances, and scheduled events.
- Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing play descriptions, reviews, and theatre-related articles, with formatting options, images, and tables.
