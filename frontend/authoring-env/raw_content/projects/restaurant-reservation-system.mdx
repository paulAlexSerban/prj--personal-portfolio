## Description:

A restaurant reservation system allows users to explore restaurants, browse menus, and make reservations. This project demonstrates your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application that effectively manages bookings and restaurant data.

## Features:

-   Data Grid: Display a list of restaurants, with relevant details such as cuisine, location, rating, and availability. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
-   Charts: Visualize restaurant-related data using various charts, including bar charts for reservation trends, line charts for customer reviews, and pie charts for cuisine distribution.
-   Scheduler: Allow users to schedule reservations, special events, and reminders related to their dining plans.
-   Date Picker: Enable users to select a date and time for filtering restaurant availability and making reservations.
-   Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing restaurant descriptions, menu items, and customer reviews, with formatting options, images, and tables.
