## Description:
An online marketplace connects buyers and sellers, allowing them to discover, compare, and purchase products. This project showcases your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application, integrating various components to enhance user experience.

## Features:

- Data Grid: Display a list of products or vendors, with relevant details such as product name, price, category, and rating. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
- Charts: Visualize marketplace data using various charts, including bar charts for sales trends, line charts for price fluctuations, and pie charts for product categories.
- Scheduler: Allow vendors to schedule promotions, restocking events, and reminders related to their inventory management.
- Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering products, orders, and sales data.
- Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing product descriptions, customer reviews, and vendor profiles, with formatting options, images, and tables.
