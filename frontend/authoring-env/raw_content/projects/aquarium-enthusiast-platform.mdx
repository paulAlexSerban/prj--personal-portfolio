## Description:
An aquarium enthusiast platform allows users to discover, learn, and share information about fishkeeping, including fish species, aquatic plants, and tank setup and maintenance tips. This project demonstrates your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application, integrating various components to enhance user experience and cater to niche interests.

## Features:

- Data Grid: Display a list of fish species, aquatic plants, and aquarium equipment, with relevant details such as name, requirements, and user ratings. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
- Charts: Visualize aquarium data using various charts, including bar charts for fish compatibility, line charts for water parameter trends, and pie charts for tank type distribution.
- Scheduler: Allow users to schedule fishkeeping events, workshops, and reminders related to aquarium maintenance and care.
- Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering aquarium events and scheduled activities.
- Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing fish species profiles, aquarium setup guides, and fishkeeping tips, with formatting options, images, and tables.
