## Description:
An inventory management system enables businesses to track their stock levels, orders, and sales. This project demonstrates your ability to create a user-friendly and interactive front-end application that effectively manages inventory data.

## Features:

- Data Grid: Display a list of products or orders, with relevant details such as item name, quantity, price, and supplier. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
- Charts: Visualize inventory data using various charts, including line charts for stock levels, bar charts for sales trends, and pie charts for product categories.
- Scheduler: Allow users to schedule stock audits, order deliveries, and reminders related to inventory management.
- Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering products, orders, and sales data.
- Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing product descriptions, order notes, and supplier communications, with formatting options, images, and tables."
