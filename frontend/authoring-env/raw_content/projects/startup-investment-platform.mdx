## Description:
A startup investment platform allows users to discover, evaluate, and connect with emerging startups seeking investment and mentorship. This project demonstrates your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application, integrating various components to enhance user experience and cater to the interests of business, investment, and banking enthusiasts.

## Features:

- Data Grid: Display a list of startups, funding rounds, and investors, with relevant details such as startup name, industry, funding amount, and investor profiles. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
- Charts: Visualize startup investment data using various charts, including bar charts for funding distribution, line charts for startup growth, and pie charts for industry distribution.
- Scheduler: Allow users to schedule pitch events, meetings, and reminders related to startup investment and mentorship.
- Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering startup events, funding rounds, and scheduled activities.
- Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing startup descriptions, investor updates, and investment opportunity articles, with formatting options, images, and tables.
