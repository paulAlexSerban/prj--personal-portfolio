## Description:
A stock market analysis platform allows users to discover, analyze, and track information about stocks, market trends, and investment strategies. This project demonstrates your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application, integrating various components to enhance user experience and cater to the interests of business, investment, and banking enthusiasts.

## Features:

- Data Grid: Display a list of stocks, indices, and market news, with relevant details such as company name, ticker symbol, price, and market cap. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
- Charts: Visualize stock market data using various charts, including bar charts for trading volume, line charts for historical prices, and pie charts for sector distribution.
- Scheduler: Allow users to schedule investment meetings, reminders, and alerts related to stock market events and announcements.
- Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering stock market data, news, and scheduled activities.
- Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing stock analysis reports, investment strategies, and market-related articles, with formatting options, images, and tables.
