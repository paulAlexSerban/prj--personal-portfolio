## Description:
A cocktail recipe and mixology platform allows users to discover, create, and share cocktail recipes, along with bartending tips and techniques. This project demonstrates your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application, integrating various components to enhance user experience and promote the world of hospitality, bartending, cocktails, and mixology.

## Features:

- Data Grid: Display a list of cocktail recipes, ingredients, and bartending techniques, with relevant details such as recipe name, difficulty, and user ratings. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
- Charts: Visualize cocktail and mixology data using various charts, including bar charts for popular ingredients, line charts for seasonal trends, and pie charts for flavor profile distribution.
- Scheduler: Allow users to schedule mixology classes, tastings, and reminders related to cocktail making and bartending events.
- Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering cocktail recipes, mixology classes, and scheduled events.
- Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing cocktail recipes, bartending tips, and mixology articles, with formatting options, images, and tables.
