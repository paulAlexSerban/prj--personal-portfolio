## Description:

An art and theatre education platform provides users with resources, courses, and tools to learn about various aspects of the performing arts. This project demonstrates your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application, integrating various components to enhance user experience and promote the world of art, actors, and theatres.

## Features:

-   Data Grid: Display a list of available courses, workshops, and resources, with relevant details such as course title, instructor, duration, and user ratings. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
-   Charts: Visualize art and theatre education data using various charts, including bar charts for course completion, line charts for learning progress, and pie charts for discipline distribution.
-   Scheduler: Allow users to schedule classes, workshops, and reminders related to their learning goals in the performing arts.
-   Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering courses, workshops, and scheduled events.
-   Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing course notes, assignment submissions, and discussion posts related to art, actors, and theatres, with formatting options, images, and tables.
