## Description:
A hospitality and bartending education platform provides users with resources, courses, and tools to learn about various aspects of the hospitality industry, including bartending, cocktails, and mixology. This project demonstrates your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application, integrating various components to enhance user experience and promote the world of hospitality, bartending, cocktails, and mixology.

## Features:

- Data Grid: Display a list of available courses, workshops, and resources, with relevant details such as course title, instructor, duration, and user ratings. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
- Charts: Visualize hospitality and bartending education data using various charts, including bar charts for course completion, line charts for learning progress, and pie charts for discipline distribution.
- Scheduler: Allow users to schedule classes, workshops, and reminders related to their learning goals in the hospitality and bartending fields.
- Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering courses, workshops, and scheduled events.
- Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing course notes, assignment submissions, and discussion posts related to hospitality, bartending, cocktails, and mixology, with formatting options, images, and tables.
