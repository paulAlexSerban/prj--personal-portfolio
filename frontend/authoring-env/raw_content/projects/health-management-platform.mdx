## Description:

A health management platform allows users to track their medical records, appointments, and health-related goals. This project showcases your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application that effectively manages health data.

## Features:

-   Data Grid: Display a list of medical records, appointments, and health goals, with relevant details such as date, description, and status. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
-   Charts: Visualize health data using various charts, including bar charts for appointment frequency, line charts for health goal progress, and pie charts for medical condition distribution.
-   Scheduler: Allow users to schedule appointments, medication reminders, and health-related tasks.
-   Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering medical records, appointments, and health goals.
-   Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing medical records, appointment notes, and health goal descriptions, with formatting options, images, and tables.
