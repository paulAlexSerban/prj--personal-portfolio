## Description:

A bartender and mixologist portfolio showcase platform allows professionals to create and maintain their online portfolios, including their work experience, signature cocktails, and bartending awards. This project showcases your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application, integrating various components to enhance user experience and promote the world of hospitality, bartending, cocktails, and mixology.

## Features:

-   Data Grid: Display a list of bartenders and mixologists, with relevant details such as name, experience, and awards. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
-   Charts: Visualize bartender and mixologist data using various charts, including bar charts for competitions and awards, line charts for career progression, and pie charts for specialty distribution.
-   Scheduler: Allow users to schedule mixology classes, workshops, and reminders related to career management and bartending opportunities.
-   Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering bartender and mixologist data, class dates, and scheduled events.
-   Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing bartender and mixologist biographies, personal statements, and project descriptions, with formatting options, images, and tables.
