# Description:

A business networking and collaboration platform allows professionals to connect, share ideas, and collaborate on projects within the realms of business, investment, and banking. This project demonstrates your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application, integrating various components to enhance user experience and cater to these specific interests."

## Features:

-   Data Grid: Display a list of professionals, companies, and projects, with relevant details such as name, industry, location, and expertise. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
-   Charts: Visualize business networking and collaboration data using various charts, including bar charts for industry distribution, line charts for collaboration trends, and pie charts for skill set distribution.
-   Scheduler: Allow users to schedule meetings, workshops, and reminders related to business networking and collaboration opportunities.
-   Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering business events, projects, and scheduled activities.
-   Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing professional profiles, project descriptions, and business-related articles, with formatting options, images, and tables.
