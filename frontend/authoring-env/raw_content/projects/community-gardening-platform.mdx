## Description:
A community gardening platform helps users connect with local gardening initiatives, share gardening tips, and plan community events. This project showcases your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application, integrating various components to enhance user experience and promote ecological awareness.

## Features:

- Data Grid: Display a list of community gardens, gardening events, and resources, with relevant details such as location, size, and contact information. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
- Charts: Visualize community gardening data using various charts, including bar charts for harvest trends, line charts for garden growth, and pie charts for plant type distribution.
- Scheduler: Allow users to schedule gardening events, workshops, and reminders related to garden maintenance and community involvement.
- Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering community gardens, events, and scheduled activities.
- Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing community garden descriptions, gardening tips, and event announcements, with formatting options, images, and tables.
