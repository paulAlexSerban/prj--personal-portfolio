## Description:

An urban green spaces exploration platform allows users to discover, rate, and share information about parks, gardens, and other green spaces in their city. This project demonstrates your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application, integrating various components to enhance user experience and promote ecological awareness.

## Features:

-   Data Grid: Display a list of urban green spaces, with relevant details such as location, size, facilities, and user ratings. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
-   Charts: Visualize green space data using various charts, including bar charts for visitor trends, line charts for facility improvements, and pie charts for green space distribution.
-   Scheduler: Allow users to schedule visits, events, and reminders related to urban green spaces.
-   Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering green spaces, events, and scheduled activities.
-   Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing green space descriptions, user reviews, and event announcements, with formatting options, images, and tables.
