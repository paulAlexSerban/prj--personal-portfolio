## Description:
A climate change awareness platform informs users about the impacts of climate change, mitigation strategies, and adaptation measures. This project demonstrates your ability to create a visually appealing and interactive front-end application, integrating various components to enhance user experience and promote ecological awareness.

## Features:

- Data Grid: Display a list of climate change articles, resources, and events, with relevant details such as title, author, and date. Allow sorting, filtering, and pagination.
- Charts: Visualize climate change data using various charts, including bar charts for greenhouse gas emissions, line charts for temperature trends, and pie charts for emission source distribution.
- Scheduler: Allow users to schedule climate change-related events, webinars, and reminders related to activism and personal action.
- Date Picker: Enable users to select a date range for filtering climate change resources and scheduling events.
- Rich Content Editor: Provide a platform for creating and editing climate change articles, personal stories, and calls to action, with formatting options, images, and tables.
